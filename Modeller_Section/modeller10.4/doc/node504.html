Excluded pairs

An 'E' line should look like: 

E Atom_index_1 Atom_index_2

where the two numeric atom indices are given (see Section 5.3.3).

For example, 

E    120 540
would exclude the nonbond interaction between atoms 120 and 540 from the list.
